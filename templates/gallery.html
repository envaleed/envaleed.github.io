About Jamaica


Jamaica is referred to as the land of wood and water. Consisting of 14 parishes, Jamaica is a mostly rural 3rd world country. In Jamaica our major avenues of revenue are from tourism and agriculture. We have many tourism hot spots such as: The Green Grotto Cave, Blue Hole, Dolphin Cove, Dunn's River Falls and White River Valley. A majority of the island's urban development is focused in the St. Andrew, St. Catherine and St. James
